Live in Harmony

Ayurveda offers timeless wisdom for daily life. Explore routines that align your body and mind with the rhythms of nature for profound well-being.

Morning: Wake before sunrise. Cleanse (tongue scraping, oil pulling). Drink warm water. Practice gentle yoga and meditation.

Midday: Eat your largest meal when digestive fire (Agni) is strongest. Take a short walk after eating.

Evening: Enjoy a light, easy-to-digest dinner. Wind down with calming activities. Avoid screens before bed.

Sleep: Aim for sleep by 10 PM to allow the body to rest and repair effectively.

Winter (Hemanta/Shishira): Favor warm, nourishing foods. Stay active to combat sluggishness. Protect yourself from cold and dampness.

Spring (Vasanta): Opt for lighter, bitter, and pungent foods to reduce Kapha. Increase activity and cleanse the body.

Summer (Grishma): Eat cooling, liquid-rich foods. Avoid excessive sun exposure. Stay hydrated with sweet, cooling drinks.

Autumn (Sharada/Varsha): Consume warm, moist, and grounding foods. Establish a regular routine to balance Vata. Practice self-oil massage (Abhyanga).
